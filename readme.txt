Generate professional, customizable POS receipts for WooCommerce orders. Perfect for retail stores, restaurants, bars, and thermal printers.

== Description ==

WooPrint Receipts transforms your WooCommerce orders into clean, professional POS receipts ready for printing. Whether you run a retail store, restaurant, bar, or any physical business using WooCommerce as your POS system, WooPrint Receipts gives you and your customers a polished receipt in seconds.

= Features =

* **One-click printing** - Print receipts directly from the WooCommerce order list or order detail page
* **Bulk printing** - Select and print multiple receipts at once
* **Frontend printing** - Customers can print receipts from the Thank You page and My Account area
* **Customizable logo** - Upload your business logo to appear on every receipt
* **Custom header/footer text** - Add promotional messages, thank you notes, or legal information
* **Configurable columns** - Choose which columns to display (ID, Quantity, Product Name, Total)
* **Product name truncation** - Set a character limit for product names (ideal for thermal printers)
* **Paper width support** - Compatible with 80mm and 58mm thermal paper
* **Shortcode support** - Use `[wooprint_receipt]` anywhere on your site
* **HPOS compatible** - Full support for WooCommerce High-Performance Order Storage
* **Print preview** - Preview receipts before printing
* **Translation ready** - English and Italian included

== Installation ==

1. Go to Plugins > Add New in your WordPress admin
2. Search for "WooPrint Receipts"
3. Click Install Now, then Activate
4. Go to WooCommerce > WooPrint Receipts to configure your settings
5. Upload your logo and customize header/footer text
6. Start printing receipts directly from your order pages

== Frequently Asked Questions ==

= Do I need any other plugin? =

No. WooPrint Receipts works standalone with WooCommerce. No additional plugins required.

= Which paper sizes are supported? =

80mm and 58mm thermal paper roll sizes are supported. You can configure this in Settings.

= Can customers print receipts? =

Yes. Customers can print receipts from the order thank you page and their My Account > Orders section.

= Is it compatible with HPOS? =

Yes. WooPrint Receipts fully supports WooCommerce High-Performance Order Storage (HPOS).

= Can I customize the receipt? =

Yes. You can upload your logo, add header/footer text, choose which columns to display, and set the paper width.
